How to write to us

You can write to us by sending your enquiry to:

Fair Work Ombudsman
GPO Box 9887
In your capital city

You can also fax your enquiry to us on:

1800 618 366

Remember to include your phone number so we can contact you.

We will respond to your enquiry within 5-7 business days.

Before you write to us

It’s a good idea to search the website to find the answer you’re looking for before you write to us.

If you can’t find the answer, then look at the Before you call page to find out what information you should include in your letter or fax.
