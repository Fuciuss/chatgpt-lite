Community assistance

We work with the community to help everyone understand their rights and responsibilities under Australian workplace laws.

To achieve this we:

  • run communication and education campaigns for industries, geographic areas, specific audience groups and issues that need attention
  • help businesses make sure they’re following Australian workplace laws
  • ask employers to pay back employees when errors are found
  • hold wages in trust for employees if they’ve left the business and can’t be contacted.
