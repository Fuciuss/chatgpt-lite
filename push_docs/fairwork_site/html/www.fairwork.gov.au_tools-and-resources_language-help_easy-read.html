Formats

We have Easy Read in 2 formats:

Webpage (HTML)

You can access Easy Read as a webpage on your computer, smart phone or tablet. This format is also called ‘HTML’.

If you use a screen reader or screen magnifier on your device, you can also use this format.

Document

You can access Easy Read as a document too. We make our information available as a PDF document.

This means you can print a copy and give it to:

  • someone who does not have access to the internet
  • a support person who will read it with you.
