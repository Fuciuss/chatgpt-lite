About the package

The Workplace relations in Australia presentation package has been designed to:

  • provide you (the lead trainer of the LSU) with foundational knowledge about Australian workplace relations
  • help you deliver the presentation to your existing or new LSU colleagues.

The purpose of the package isn’t for LSU staff to become Australian workplace relations experts. The package is designed to:

  • equip LSU staff with essential workplace relations knowledge so they can help prepare Pacific Australia Labour Mobility (PALM) scheme employees before they arrive in Australia
  • provide information about referring matters to us.

The package includes a presentation, fact sheet and survey. You can find more information about these and download them below.

Download the package

Please download the package each time you deliver the presentation so that you can be confident you’re using the latest version. We will continue to update the presentation as needed over time.

Download the Workplace relations in Australia presentation .

These slides have detailed speaking notes to help you deliver the presentation. The presentation covers 4 key topics:

  1. About the Fair Work Ombudsman
  2. The Fair Work Ombudsman’s role in Pacific Australia Labour Mobility
  3. Workplace laws in Australia
  4. Getting help from the Fair Work Ombudsman.

Download the Overview and resources – workplace relations in Australia fact sheet .

This is an accompanying resource that you can give to LSU staff. The fact sheet contains information about how to contact us and provides links to our relevant tools and resources.

Download the Workplace relations in Australia presentation survey .

Please give this survey to attendees of your presentation after you finish presenting. The survey will allow LSU staff to provide feedback on the presentation package.

The survey has been created in two formats. You can choose to provide a link to the Qualtrics Survey above or download a printable PDF version . Please send completed PDF surveys to pacificlabourscheme@fwo.gov.au .

Using the package

Remember your presentation shouldn’t be recorded. This is to prevent outdated information being stored by participants.
