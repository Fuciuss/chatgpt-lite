Calculators

Our Pay and Conditions Tool is made up of 4 calculators that help you calculate:

  • pay rates including penalty rates and allowances
  • shift calculations - a sum of pay rates for up to 4 weeks of shifts
  • annual and sick leave entitlements
  • notice and redundancy entitlements.

Website translator

You can translate most of our website and online resources with our built-in website translator. It translates into over 30 different languages and is quick and simple to use.

How to use the translator:

  1. In the top right-hand corner of the webpage, click on the language drop-down box.

  2. Select your preferred language from the menu.

  3. In the pop up box, choose to:
    1. translate the whole website into another language (selecting this option will mean other pages will automatically translate)
    2. translate the page you're on only.

Templates

Sometimes you need to write a letter or email to help sort out a workplace problem. Finding the right words can be difficult. Our templates can help to get you started.

The most relevant templates for fixing workplace problems are:

  • Request for records template tool
  • Managing underperformance templates .

Our templates cover a range of topics. They can all can be tailored to suit your situation. Some templates are interactive. They use our template tool which will ask you simple questions and use your answers to tailor a letter for you. You will be able to save and edit your letter at the end. Other templates are downloadable documents you can edit.

For a full list, visit our Templates page.

Online learning courses

Our online courses will teach you skills and strategies to help you at work. The most relevant courses for fixing workplace problems are:

  • Difficult conversations in the workplace – managers course
  • Difficult conversations in the workplace – employee course .

The courses are free and interactive. They take around 20 minutes to complete. You can access them from your computer and most tablets and mobiles.

These courses help managers and employees gain the skills and confidence to discuss workplace issues when they arise. They include:

  • information about how to handle a difficult conversation
  • tips to help you prepare, manage your emotions and reach a positive outcome
  • interactive scenarios to help you practice your conversation skills
  • downloadable resources and links to further information.

For a full list of our award-winning online courses for employers, employees and business advisers, visit our Online learning centre .
