Alerts and updates

Sign up if you'd like to receive alerts and updates on topics relevant to you, including:

  • pay and award changes
  • important industry updates
  • changes to workplace laws
  • our employer newsletter
  • media releases.

New subscribers

When you sign up, you can choose the information that you would like to receive.

To subscribe, simply follow these steps:

  • go to the Subscription centre
  • enter your name and email address
  • select the information you would like to receive from us
  • click the subscribe button at the end of the selection.

Existing subscribers

If you’re an existing subscriber, follow these steps to manage your email preferences:

  • go to Manage preferences
  • enter your email address
  • click the submit button.

We’ll then send you an email with a personalised link. This will allow you to update your preferences or unsubscribe from our emails.

Privacy statement

We use Swift Digital, an online service provider to send and manage emails. We’re committed to protecting your privacy, all information collected using the Swift Digital service is the property of FWO and is never shared or used by third parties. We'll only use your personal information to send email updates about the topics you have subscribed to. For more information, go to our Privacy page.
