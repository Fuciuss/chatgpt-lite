2021 - 2022 litigation outcomes

This section provides information on our litigation outcomes for the 2021 - 2022 financial year.

We use legal activities as one way to achieve compliance with Australian workplace laws. These activities are part of a broader compliance system that makes sure employers and employees comply with these laws.
