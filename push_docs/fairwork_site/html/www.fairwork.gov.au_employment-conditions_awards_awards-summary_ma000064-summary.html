Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award [MA000064]

Who the Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award covers

The Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award covers field geologists and their employers.

Examples of employees covered by the Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award include:

  • trainee mudloggers
  • competent mudloggers
  • senior mudloggers
  • data engineers.

The Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award also covers labour hire businesses and their employees who are placed with an organisation as field geologists.

Check the award for full information on who is covered and the classification descriptions.

Source reference: Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award [MA000064] clauses 4, 10 and schedule A

Who the Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award doesn’t cover

The following employers and employees are not covered by the Hydrocarbons Field Geologists Award:

  • clerical and administrative employees
  • employees who work in the hydrocarbons industry that are not field geologists such as:
  • services employees
  • onshore drilling employees
  • offshore drilling employees
  • operations and processing employees
  • maintenance trades and modification employees.
  • employees who work in oil refineries and the manufacturing industry.

Check the award for full information on coverage and classifications.
