Counter service and appointments at Fair Work Ombudsman offices due to COVID-19

Counter service and appointments continue to be unavailable at all of our offices due to COVID-19.

You can instead contact us by:

  • sending an online enquiry
  • calling us .

Most of your workplace relations questions can be answered on our website or over the phone but in some cases we may ask you to come into one of our offices to meet with us face to face.

We have offices in all capital cities and 14 regional locations across Australia. If you've submitted an online enquiry and we need to meet with you face to face we will book in a time for you to come and see us at one of these locations.
